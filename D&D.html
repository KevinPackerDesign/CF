D&D Classes

  • Artificer

    • An Artificer uses magic infused objects as weapons or to cast spells. They are very crafty in their ways.

  • Barbarian

    • A Barbarian uses weapons, normally an axe or warhammer, to wail on an enemy. They use there rage to make the attacks stronger.

  • Bard

    • A Bard uses an instrument or a song or dance to heal, inspire or attack. They keep the party entertained.

  • Blood Hunter

    • A Blood Hunter is a spell caster and fighter that sacrafices part of themselves to inflict curses or deal extra damage.

  • Cleric

    • A Cleric is a Healer, very handy to have with you, but dont underestimate their power if you are evil.

  • Druid

    • A Druid is one with nature. They tend to take on charateristic of thier favorit area. Carefull of the critters around you if you have upset a Druid, that chipmuck might be their friend.

  • Fighter

    • A Fighter is normally a soldier or mercenary. They hold to their belifes and 'live or die by the sword.' They can use any weapon. Just when you think they are down for the count they may get a second wind and come back swinging.

  • Monk

    • A Monk may be peacefull at heart, of they can love a good fight. Never take them for granted, unarmed or not a Monk is not someone to triffle with.

  • Paladin

    • A Paladin has mad a pact with their god. They get there strength from their faith. Even though they can deal damage, they can heal as well. They protect those they love with their life.

  • Ranger

    • A Ranger prefers to keep their distance, keeping an eye out for anything suspisious. Like a Druid they are linked to nature, they tend to travel with an animal companion

  • Rogue

    • A Rogue is master or shawdows. They can disapper right infront of you, and surprise you from behind. Keep on a Rogues good side or you may regret it.

  • Sorcerer

    • A Sorcerer, gets their magic from ancestory or from an event in their life that made magic flow through their blood. Sometimes a Sorcerer can be a trickster or at times they can pure. Just be sure to find out which.

  • Warlock

    • A Warlock has struck a deal with a God or a Fiend to get their magic, be sure to find out which before making friends with them.

  • Wizard

    • A Wizard gets their magic through practice and study, years of hard work have made them into expert wielders of the arcane arts.


    There are many more that are custom classes. The ones that I have listed are just the ones that are mainly used. If you want to read more about D&D classes check out the Wiki.
